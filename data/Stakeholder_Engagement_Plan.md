    # Stakeholder Engagement Plan
    
    ## Project
    Stakeholder Gap Detector Pilot
    
    ## Engagement Objectives
    The project will maintain stakeholder alignment, reduce delivery risk, and ensure readiness for rollout across operations, customer support, and governance teams.
    
    ## Stakeholder Engagement Strategies
    
    ### Maria Chen — Executive Sponsor
    - Engagement approach: Manage closely.
    - Actions:
      - Weekly steering committee update with progress, milestones, and delivery risks.
      - Monthly budget review.
      - Escalation path for critical delivery blockers.
    - Desired outcome:
      - Continued sponsorship and rapid decision-making support.
    
    ### David Okafor — Operations Lead
    - Engagement approach: Manage closely.
    - Actions:
      - Bi-weekly operations working session.
      - Review rollout impacts before release.
      - Share deployment checklist one week before go-live.
    - Desired outcome:
      - Operational readiness and low disruption during rollout.
    
    ### Priya Nair — IT Security Manager
    - Engagement approach: Keep satisfied.
    - Actions:
      - Monthly security review meeting.
      - Share architecture summary before production deployment.
    - Desired outcome:
      - Security approval before release.
    
    ### Jonas Weber — Change Manager
    - Engagement approach: Keep informed.
    - Actions:
      - Weekly change and communications sync.
      - Prepare adoption messaging for impacted teams.
      - Track training completion metrics.
    - Desired outcome:
      - Smooth adoption and clear stakeholder communication.
    
    ### Fatima Hassan — Customer Support Manager
    - Engagement approach: Keep informed.
    - Actions:
      - Bi-weekly support readiness review.
      - Share knowledge base updates for support agents.
    - Desired outcome:
      - Support team prepared for user questions after launch.
    
    ## General Communication Cadence
    - Steering committee: Weekly
    - Operations sync: Bi-weekly
    - Security review: Monthly
    - Change forum: Weekly
    - Support review: Bi-weekly
    
    ## Notes
    - Compliance engagement will be handled through existing governance channels.
    - Additional stakeholders may be added later if they become relevant.
